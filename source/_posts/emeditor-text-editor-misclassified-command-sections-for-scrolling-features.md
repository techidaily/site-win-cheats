The 12 Scroll commands (Scroll Left Edge, etc.) belong to \[Window\] category.  
But they are “labeled” \[Edit\] category. (See them in the Keyboard Map.)  
This causes, in the Keyboard tab of the Configuration Properties dialog, pressing the > or < button can not reach these commands. And the commands after them in \[Window\] category (e.g. Last Accessed Document) are also affected.
